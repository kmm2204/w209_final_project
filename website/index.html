Why have we done this?

The census is important

Census data is used to:

  • Determine the distribution of Congressional seats to states.
  • make planning decisions about community services
  • Distribute over $400 billion funds to local, state and tribal governments

The current administration seeks to undermine the census

  • Thomas Brunell, head of the census, has a history of supporting racially driven gerrymandering
  • The inclusion of citizenship questions and removal of identity questions will have a chilling effect on response rates from minority and immigrant communities
  • These changes will effectively siphon resources away from taxpayers

Our target audience

This visualization is intended for funders, policy makers, and nonprofit organizations to resist the current administration and serve those who would otherwise go uncounted.
